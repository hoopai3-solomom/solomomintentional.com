PGT-A Genetic Testing

PGT-A identifies embryos with the correct number of chromosomes (euploid).

Benefits

  • Higher chance of implantation
  • Lower miscarriage risk
  • Higher chance of a healthy pregnancy
  • Helps choose the embryo with the best likelihood of success
Important: PGT-A does not guarantee a 100% healthy baby, but it improves the chance of a successful pregnancy by selecting the healthiest embryo.
